Stochastic Calculus

Daily Math Fact

In Itô calculus, the Itô integral of a stochastic process $X_t$ with respect to a Wiener process $W_t$ is defined as $\int_0^t X_s \, dW_s = \lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{i=0}^{n-1} X_{t_i^n} (W_{t_{i+1}^n} - W_{t_i^n})$ where $0 = t_0^n < t_1^n < \, \cdots \, < t_n^n = t$ is a partition.
